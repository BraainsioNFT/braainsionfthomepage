So these NFT skins will be bought with crypto?

Yes NFT skins will be bought with crypto. Think of it rather than "NFT skin" but instead a NFT that is a skin ingame (since there will be more than just getting a skin to buying braainsNFT). The price will be about 1-1.5 SOL depending on market conditions.
